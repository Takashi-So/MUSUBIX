## 2. 機能要件（EARS形式）

### 2.1 会員管理

#### REQ-GYM-001: 会員登録
**[Ubiquitous]**
THE system SHALL allow registration of new members with personal information, membership plan, and emergency contact.

#### REQ-GYM-002: 会員認証
**[Event-Driven]**
WHEN a member scans their membership card at the entrance,
THE system SHALL verify membership status and grant or deny access within 2 seconds.

#### REQ-GYM-003: 会員プラン管理
**[State-Driven]**
WHILE a membership is active,
THE system SHALL track remaining sessions, expiration date, and usage history.

#### REQ-GYM-004: 会員情報更新禁止
**[Unwanted]**
THE system SHALL NOT allow modification of membership plan without proper authorization.

### 2.2 予約管理

#### REQ-GYM-005: クラス予約
**[Event-Driven]**
WHEN a member requests to book a fitness class,
THE system SHALL check availability and confirm or waitlist the booking.

#### REQ-GYM-006: パーソナルトレーニング予約
**[Event-Driven]**
WHEN a member requests a personal training session,
THE system SHALL match available trainers and propose time slots.

#### REQ-GYM-007: キャンセルポリシー
**[Optional]**
IF a booking is cancelled less than 24 hours before the scheduled time,
THEN THE system SHALL apply a cancellation fee according to the policy.

#### REQ-GYM-008: 予約リマインダー
**[Event-Driven]**
WHEN a booking is 24 hours away,
THE system SHALL send a reminder notification to the member.

### 2.3 トレーナー管理

#### REQ-GYM-009: トレーナー登録
**[Ubiquitous]**
THE system SHALL maintain trainer profiles including certifications, specializations, and availability.

#### REQ-GYM-010: スケジュール管理
**[State-Driven]**
WHILE a trainer is on shift,
THE system SHALL display their real-time availability and assigned sessions.

#### REQ-GYM-011: パフォーマンス追跡
**[Ubiquitous]**
THE system SHALL track trainer performance metrics including session completion rate and member feedback.

### 2.4 施設・設備管理

#### REQ-GYM-012: 設備予約
**[Event-Driven]**
WHEN a member requests to use specific equipment,
THE system SHALL check availability and reserve for a time slot.

#### REQ-GYM-013: メンテナンス管理
**[Event-Driven]**
WHEN equipment requires maintenance,
THE system SHALL mark it as unavailable and notify staff.

#### REQ-GYM-014: 混雑状況表示
**[Ubiquitous]**
THE system SHALL display real-time gym occupancy and equipment availability.

### 2.5 支払い・課金

#### REQ-GYM-015: 自動課金
**[Event-Driven]**
WHEN a membership renewal date arrives,
THE system SHALL process automatic payment and update membership status.

#### REQ-GYM-016: 支払い履歴
**[Ubiquitous]**
THE system SHALL maintain complete payment history for each member.

#### REQ-GYM-017: 未払い処理
**[Optional]**
IF payment fails for 3 consecutive attempts,
THEN THE system SHALL suspend membership and notify the member.

### 2.6 レポート・分析

#### REQ-GYM-018: 利用統計
**[Ubiquitous]**
THE system SHALL generate daily, weekly, and monthly usage reports.

#### REQ-GYM-019: 収益レポート
**[Ubiquitous]**
THE system SHALL calculate and display revenue metrics by membership type and service.

#### REQ-GYM-020: 会員動向分析
**[Ubiquitous]**
THE system SHALL analyze member retention, churn rate, and engagement patterns.
